0. Segment Information
VMware operates in one reportable operating segment, thus all required ﬁnancial segment information can be found on the consolidated financial statements.
Operating segments are deﬁned as components of an enterprise for which separate financial information is evaluated regularly by the chief operating decision
maker in deciding how to allocate resources and assessing performance. VMware’s chief operating decision maker allocates resources and assesses performance
based upon discrete financial information at the consolidated level.
