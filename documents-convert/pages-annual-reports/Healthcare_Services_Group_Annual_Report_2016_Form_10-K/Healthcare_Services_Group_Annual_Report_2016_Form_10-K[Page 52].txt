Note 10— Other Employee Beneﬁt Plans
Retirement Savings Plan
Since October 1, 1999, we have had a retirement savings plan for eligible employees (the “RSP”) under Section 401(k) of the Internal Revenue Code. The RSP
allows eligible employees to contribute up to 15% of their eligible compensation on a pre-tax basis. There is no match by the Company.
