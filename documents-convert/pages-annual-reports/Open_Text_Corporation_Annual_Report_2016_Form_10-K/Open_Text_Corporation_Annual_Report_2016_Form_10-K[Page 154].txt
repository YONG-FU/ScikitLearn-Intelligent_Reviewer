Purchase of an Asset Group Constituting a Business
On April 30, 2016, we acquired certain customer experience software and services assets and liabilities from HP Inc.
(CEM Business) for approximately $160.0 million, of which $7.3 million is currently held back and unpaid in accordance with
the terms of the purchase agreement. In accordance with Topic 805, this acquisition was accounted for as a business
combination. We believe this acquisition will complement our current software portfolio, particularly our Customer Experience
Management and Cloud oﬁerings.
The results of operations of this acquisition have been consolidated with those of OpenText beginning April 30, 2016.
