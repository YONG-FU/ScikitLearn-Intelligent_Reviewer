Acquisition of Cooperativa Central de Pesquisa Agricola's Seed Business
On January 30, 2015, Dow AgroSciences LLC ("DAS") acquired Cooperativa Central de Pesquisa Agricola's ("Coodetec") seed
business for $169 million, of which $121 million was paid in 2015, $24 million was paid in 2016 and the remaining portion
will be paid by the end of the first quarter of 20 17. The acquisition of Coodetec's seed business is expected to advance the
development of Dow AgroSciences' soybean program and strengthen the Company’s position in the corn market segment.
