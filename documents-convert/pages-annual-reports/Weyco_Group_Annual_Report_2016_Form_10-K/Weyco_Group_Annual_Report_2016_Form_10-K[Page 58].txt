13. COMMITMENTS
The Company operates retail shoe stores under both short-term and long-term leases. Leases
provide for a minimum rental plus percentage rentals based upon sales in excess of a specified
amount. The Company also leases office space in the U.S. and its distribution facilities in Canada
and overseas. Total minimum rents were $9.8 million in 2016, $9.1 million in 2015 and $9.7 million in
2014. Percentage rentals were $441,000 in 2016, $461,000 in 2015, and $512,000 in 2014.
