Sam's Club Segment
The Sam's Club segment operates membership-only warehouse clubs, as well as samsclub.com, in the US. and had net sales of $56.8 billion , $58.0 billion and
$57.2 billion for fiscal 2016 , 2015 and 2014 , respectively. During the most recent fiscal year, no single club location accounted for as much as 1% of total
Company net sales.
