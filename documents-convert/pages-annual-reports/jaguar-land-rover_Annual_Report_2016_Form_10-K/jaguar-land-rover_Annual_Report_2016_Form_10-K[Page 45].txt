OFFSETTING
Certain ﬁnancial assets and ﬁnancial liabilities are subject to offsetting where there is currently a legally enforceable right
to set off recognised amounts and the Group intends to either settle on a net basis, or to realise the asset and settle the
liability simultaneously.
Derivative ﬁnancial assets and ﬁnancial liabilities are subject to master netting arrangements whereby in the case of
insolvency, derivative ﬁnancial assets and ﬁnancial liabilities can be settled on a net basis.
