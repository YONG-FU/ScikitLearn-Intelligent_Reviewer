BUSINESS OVERVIEW
We are a leading developer of technology-based products and services and associated content for the worldwide
gaming, lottery and interactive gaming industries. Our portfolio includes gaming machines and game content, casino
management systems, table game products and services, instant and draw-based lottery games, lottery systems, lottery content
and services, interactive gaming and social casino solutions as well as other products and services. We also gain access to
technologies and pursue global expansion through strategic acquisitions and equity investments.
