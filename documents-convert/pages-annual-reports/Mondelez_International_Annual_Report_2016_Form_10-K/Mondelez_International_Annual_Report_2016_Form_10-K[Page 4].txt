PART I
Item 1. Business.
General
We are one of the world’s largest snack companies with global net revenues of $25.9 billion and net earnings of $1 .7 billion in 2016. We manufacture
and market delicious snack food and beverage products for consumers in approximately 165 countries around the world. Our portfolio includes many
iconic snack brands including Nabisco , Oreo, LU and belVita biscuits ; Cadbury, Milka, Cadbury Daily Milk and Toblerone chocolate; Trident gum;
Halls candy and Tang powdered beverages.
We are proud members of the Standard and Poor’s 500, NASDAQ 100 and Dow Jones Sustainability Index. Our Common Stock trades on The
NASDAQ Global Select Market under the symbol “MDLZ.” We have been incorporated in the Commonwealth of Virginia since 2000.
