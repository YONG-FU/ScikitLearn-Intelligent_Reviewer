or other amount paid or payable by the purchasing Lender in respect of the total amount so recovered. Each Borrower agrees that any Lender so purchasing a
participation from another Lender pursuant to this Section 2.16 may, to the fullest extent permitted by law, exercise all its rights of payment (including the right of
set-oft) with reSpect to such participation as fully as if such Lender were the direct creditor of such Borrower in the amount of such participation.
