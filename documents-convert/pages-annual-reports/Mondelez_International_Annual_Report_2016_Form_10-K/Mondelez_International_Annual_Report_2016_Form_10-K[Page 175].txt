SECTION 703. Reports by Trustee.
(a) Within 60 days after May 15 of each year, the Trustee for the Securities of each series shall send to each Holder of the Securities of such series entitled to
receive reports pursuant to Section 704(3), a brief report dated as of such date that complies with Section 313(a) of the Trust Indenture Act. The Trustee for the
Securities of each series shall also comply with Sections 313(b), 313(0) and 313(d) of the Trust Indenture Act.
(b) At the time that the Trustee for the Securities of each series mails such a report to the Holders of Securities of such series, each such Trustee shall ﬁle a
copy of that report with the Commission and with each stock exchange on which the Securities of that series are listed. The Company shall provide prompt written
notice to the appropriate Trustee when the Securities of any series are listed on any stock exchange and of any delisting thereof.
