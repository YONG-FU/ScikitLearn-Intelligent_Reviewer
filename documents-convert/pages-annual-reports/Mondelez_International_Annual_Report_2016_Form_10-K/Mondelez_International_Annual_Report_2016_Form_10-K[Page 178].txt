supplemental hereto for the purpose of adding any provisions to or changing in any manner or eliminating any of the provisions of this Indenture or of modifying in
any manner the rights of the Holders of such Securities under this Indenture, but only with the consent of the Holders of more than 50% in aggregate principal
amount of the Outstanding Securities of each series of Securities then Outstanding affected thereby, in each case by Act of said Holders of Securities of each such
series delivered to the Company and the Trustee for Securities of each such series; provided, however, that no such supplemental indenture shall, without the
consent of the Holder of each Outstanding Security affected thereby:
