Biographical Information
Jack Yun MA (£573) is our lead founder and, since May 2013, has served as our executive chairman. From our
founding in 1999 and until May 2013, Jack served as our chairman and chief executive officer. He is also the
founder of the Zhejiang-based Jack Ma Foundation. Jack currently serves on the board of SoftBank Group Corp.,
one of our major shareholders and a Japanese corporation listed on the Tokyo Stock Exchange. He is also a
member of the Foundation Board of the World Economic Forum, a member of the UK. government’s Business
Advisory Group, chairman of the Zhejiang Chamber of Commerce, as well as chairman of the China Entrepreneur
Club. In January 2016, he was named a Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) advocate by the United Nations.
Jack graduated from Hangzhou Teacher’s Institute with a major in English language education.
