Market and Dividend Information
P&G has been paying a dividend for 126 consecutive years since its original incorporation in 1890 and has increased its dividend for 60 consecutive
years. Over the past five years, the dividend has increased at an annual compound average rate of 5%. Nevertheless, as in the past, further
dividends will be considered after reviewing dividend yields, profitability expectations and financing needs and will be declared at the discretion of
the Company's Board of Directors.
